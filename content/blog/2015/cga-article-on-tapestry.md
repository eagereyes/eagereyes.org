# Link: CG&A Article on Tapestry

I've written [a short piece about the Tapestry conference](http://online.qmags.com/CGA0315#pg14&amp;mode2) for the *Graphically Speaking* column in *Computer Graphics and Applications*. The article talks about the reasoning behind [Tapestry](http://www.tapestryconference.com), how it's different from academic conferences, and gives a few examples of talks. It even includes anecdotal evidence to show that the conference has enabled actual knowledge transfer.

If you [prefer a PDF](/publications/Kosara-CGA-2015) to the e-reader version, you can find that in my publications.
