# Plot.io

[Data visualization startup Plot.io](http://www.plot.io/?lrRef=2AEnO) has been making some noise lately. From what I know so far, it looks a lot like Tableau, but presumably works in the browser. This could be a potential [successor to Swivel](/criticism/the-rise-and-fall-of-swivel), which [folded a bit over a year ago](/criticism/swivel-part-2-solving-a-single-problem), and maybe what [Verifiable](/blog/2010/end-of-verifiable-com) was trying to do.

[Their teaser video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q904idCrY80) is a bit confusing, but what it shows is an interface that looks a lot like Tableau. On [their Twitter feed](http://twitter.com/plot_io), they also talk about marks, and they seem to be aware of what is going on in the visualization community. They're certainly worth watching.
